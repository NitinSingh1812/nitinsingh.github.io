Income Tax Slabs FAQ 2025-26 (India)

Complete Guide to New Tax Regime & Revised Slabs

Tax Regime Comparison (2024 vs 2025)

The 2025 new tax regime offers a broader tax-free limit of ₹12 lakh with lower rates for higher-income ranges. The old regime remains unchanged. Compared to 2024, the 2025 new tax slabs improve tax efficiency, especially for middle-income earners, by lowering tax burdens on incomes above ₹12 lakh.

What has changed in the 2025 new tax regime?

The tax-free income limit increased to ₹12 lakh, and lower tax rates for higher income slabs were introduced.

Is the old tax regime better than the new one?

The old regime is better for individuals with many exemptions and deductions, while the new regime is better for those without them.

What are the main benefits of the 2025 tax regime?

The main benefits include a higher tax-free limit (₹12 lakh), simplified tax calculation without deductions, and generally lower tax rates for most income brackets.

Who benefits most from the 2025 tax changes?

Middle-income earners, especially those with annual income between ₹12 lakh to ₹24 lakh, benefit most from the new tax regime due to reduced tax rates and higher tax-free limit.

Basics of Income Tax

Income tax is a crucial part of India's tax system. Here are the key points:

  • It's a direct tax on income earned by individuals and businesses
  • Tax year runs from April 1st to March 31st
  • Different income sources are taxed: salary, business, investments, etc.
  • Tax rates vary based on income slabs
  • Both old and new tax regimes are currently available

What is the new tax regime in India for 2025-26?

The new tax regime eliminates income tax for earnings up to ₹12 lakh and introduces lower tax rates for different income ranges.

What are the revised income tax slabs under the new regime?

• ₹0 - ₹4,00,000: No tax
• ₹4,00,001 - ₹8,00,000: 5%
• ₹8,00,001 - ₹12,00,000: 10%
• ₹12,00,001 - ₹16,00,000: 15%
• ₹16,00,001 - ₹20,00,000: 20%
• ₹20,00,001 - ₹24,00,000: 25%
• ₹24,00,001 and above: 30%

How does the old tax regime compare to the new one?

The old regime offers more exemptions but has higher rates, while the new regime offers lower rates with fewer deductions.

What is income tax?

Income tax is a tax levied by the government on the income earned by individuals and businesses.

What is the difference between the old and new tax regimes?

The old regime allows deductions like HRA and 80C, while the new regime offers lower tax rates without deductions.

How do I calculate my income tax?

Tax is calculated based on your total annual income, which is then taxed according to applicable slabs.

What is a tax slab?

A tax slab is a range of income where a specific tax rate is applied.

Which tax regime is better for me?

If you have multiple deductions, the old regime might benefit you. Otherwise, the new regime with lower rates might be simpler.
